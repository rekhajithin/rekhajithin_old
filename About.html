Dr Rekha Jithin is an Ayurvedic medical doctor by background with proven experience of more than 10 years in lifestyle and integrative medicine, holistic health and nutrition.

"My goal is to make 'being healthy' simple and helping people achieve optimal health. I believe in the power of lifestyle as a magic pill to being healthy and my treatment focuses on the four pillars of health viz. nutrition, exercise, sleep and relaxation. As doctors we are largely taught to 'cure' illnesses by symptomatic treatments. The good news is that there is a proven way to treat illnesses succesfully - treating patients holistically and treats them as individuals."

Dr Rekha has executed many successful workplace wellness programmes for multinational companies and has helped people manage diseases through lifestyle. Over the course of her career she has routinely helped patients manage

Reverse Type 2 Diabetes
Lower blood pressure without medicines
Reduce weight, manage obesity
Reduce symptoms of menopause naturally
Sleep better and harmonise circadian rhythm
Eliminate irritable bowel syndrome
PCOS, Hypothyroidism and women's health problems

"My goal is to empower you to be your own health architect. When you are healthy, you feel better, you feel happier and live more!"  - Dr Rekha Jithin
