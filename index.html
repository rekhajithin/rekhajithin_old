The Four Pillars of Health

My goal is to change the way we look at 'being healthy'. Lifestyle is the ultimate drug to prevent and manage disease. A 360 degree approach on health with focus on sleep, stress, exercise and nutrition has shown best results in reversal and management of patients with chronic illnesses like diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, cancer etc and also for preventing the onset of diseases, improving general health and wellness

Eat Well. 
Did you kow? You can eat your way to better health! Mother nature has an edible pharmacy which is accessible to everyone

Sleep Well. 
Sleep is one of the most underrated pillars of health. A good quality sleep of 7 to 8 hours is key to good health

Move More. 
The right combination of cardio and strength training goes a long way in helping you build a well toned and fit body

Relax More. 
Its high time we dedicate time every day to relax. Taking 10 minutes of 'Me- time' everyday helps manage stress
